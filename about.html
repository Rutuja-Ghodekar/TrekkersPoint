More About Us

Trekkers point was founded in the year 2019 . Our first trek was Purandar Fort in Pune district. The group was only of 8 people when we started ,but now there are nearly 50 members in the group . We often host campaign of fort cleanliness , so that our beauty of forts can be preserved .
